Sustainability

YourProject reduces environmental impact across the product lifecycle. Key approaches include:

  • Energy-efficient design: Minimal client-side work and optimized assets to reduce device and server energy use.
  • Sustainable hosting: Prefer providers with renewable energy commitments and small carbon footprints.
  • Resource minimization: Small bundle sizes, efficient images, and lazy-loading to save bandwidth and power.
  • Longevity: Maintainable architecture and clear documentation to keep the product useful longer with less churn.
